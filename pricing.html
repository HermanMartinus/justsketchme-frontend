FAQ

How does subscribing work?

You will be issued a license key after payment (it will also be emailed to you). You can then copy and paste the key into the app to activate all the features in the app.

Can I use this license key on multiple devices?

The same license key can be used on all of your devices. All devices using the same license key will share saved poses and scenes.

Is there a one-time payment option?

We have a lifetime access option on Gumroad for $199 (once-off payment).

How do I download the app?

Once you have subscribed, the download instructions will be available in the settings menu (top right) of the web app.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time by going to your Gumroad library and selecting "Cancel subscription".
